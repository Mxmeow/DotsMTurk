Experimental Instructions

Click "Next" to see the next step appear on top!

Step 1: Fixate on the center point of the screen. For the entire experiment you should be focused on the fixation point.

Step 2: A series of dots will start flashing on your screen. There are two categories in which these dots fall into: red and blue.

Step 3: Try and keep track of all the dots and their average orientation.

Step 4: After 24 trials we will ask you to distingush what is the mean orientation for each of the two colors. The order of the color you will be asked to locate for is in the top lefthand corner. The cycle will repeat for 36 blocks.
